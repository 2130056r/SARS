SARS User Guide

Creating a Query

When you first begin a search box will be shown on screen from here you can simply add query terms by typing them into the search field and clicking search this will then display abstracts of documents that share a topic with your search

Relevant Abstracts

Once the abstracts are on screen each will have it's own checkbox. To deem an abstract as relevant reading information simply check the boxes of those you find a common subject with your initial searches.

Relevant Documents

after selecting all the relevant abstracts and continuing from here you are taken to a new page that will supply you with the documents of each of the abstracts you have chosen as relevant. From here you can, again choose which of the documents contain relevant information and which do not by means of the checkbox system. Once complete you have now finished your first systematic review and are now left with a list of documents that will supply other users with relevant information in a much faster time than you did, you are a good person for doing this.
